# Marketplace Commerce Readiness

Checkout is enabled only after the marketplace is active, visible, passes the launch checklist, and has all of the following real regional records:

- At least one active marketplace product price in the marketplace currency.
- At least one active warehouse assigned to the marketplace and its country.
- At least one active stock row routed through that marketplace and country.
- At least one active tax zone for the marketplace, either country-specific or an intentional marketplace fallback.
- At least one active delivery zone for the marketplace, either country-specific or an intentional marketplace fallback.

The readiness service is read-only. It never creates commercial data, estimates taxes, invents delivery terms, or enables checkout automatically.
